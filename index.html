About Me

I’m Derick Ogutu, an Electrical and Electronics Engineering graduate specialized in Instrumentation and Control. I build and troubleshoot systems that blend electronics, embedded firmware, smart automation, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning — with real-world experience in vehicle electronics, industrial maintenance, and heavy-duty systems. At Data Integrated Ltd., I deploy and diagnise GPS, GSM, and embedded telematics in vehicles. At Unibrain Industries, I supported preventive maintenance of control panels, motor drives, and sensor systems in an industrial setting. My attachment at Kenya Power exposed me to SCADA systems, grid-level diagnostics, and field instrumentation. I’m skilled in STM32/ESP32 microcontrollers & PCB design (KiCad), electrical schematics, Industrial Automation, Electrical Systems troubleshooting & Preventive Maintenance, sensor calibration, C/C++ and Python. I also hold certifications in Software Engineering (ALX), Web Development (PLP) and AI/ML (Oracle University). Whether working on a rugged system or cloud-integrated IoT device, I thrive at solving real-world problems with smart, scalable tech — especially in the fields of electromobility, industrial control, and energy systems.
